Our Story

Born from the streets, built for the streets

From Observation to Innovation

It all started when our founder, Rajesh, noticed his neighborhood vendor, Ramesh Uncle, struggling with rising ingredient costs and unreliable suppliers. Despite serving the most delicious pav bhaji in the area, Ramesh Uncle was spending hours each morning at wholesale markets, often returning with subpar ingredients at inflated prices.

This sparked an idea: What if we could bring the wholesale market to the vendors? What if technology could solve the age-old problems of quality, cost, and convenience that plague our street food heroes?

After months of research, talking to hundreds of vendors across Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore, we realized this wasn't just Ramesh Uncle's problem—it was a systemic issue affecting thousands of street food entrepreneurs across India.

Thus, StreetEats Connect was born—not in a boardroom, but on the streets, from real conversations with real people who pour their hearts into feeding our communities.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our mission to transform street food ecosystem

2022

The Spark

Rajesh meets Ramesh Uncle and identifies the core problem. Extensive market research begins across Mumbai's street food hubs. Initial concept validation with 200+ vendors.

2023

Foundation Laid

Team formation and MVP development. Pilot program launched with 50 vendors in Mumbai. First partnerships established with local suppliers. Initial funding secured.

2024

Rapid Growth

Platform expansion to Delhi and Bangalore. Group buying feature launched. PM SVANidhi integration completed. Crossed 1,000 active vendors milestone.
