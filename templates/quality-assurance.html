Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our quality assurance system

How do you verify supplier credentials?

We conduct a thorough verification process that includes checking FSSAI certification, business registration, physical location verification, and background checks. We also collect and verify references from existing customers.

What happens if I receive poor quality materials?

Our platform has a robust issue resolution system. You can report quality issues immediately through the app with photos. We offer a 100% quality guarantee, meaning you'll receive a replacement or refund for any substandard products within 24 hours.

How does the rapid testing kit work?

Our affordable testing kits are designed for easy use by vendors without technical training. They can detect common adulterants in milk, ghee, oils, and spices through simple color-change tests. Each kit comes with clear instructions and can perform multiple tests.

What is blockchain traceability and when will it be available?

Blockchain traceability creates an immutable record of each product's journey from source to vendor. This technology will be implemented in phases starting next quarter, beginning with high-value items like saffron and specialty ingredients before expanding to all products.

How can I participate in community quality control?

After signing up, you can join or create a local vendor group in your area. These groups coordinate quality checks, share feedback, and can organize joint supplier visits. The app will notify you of community quality check events happening nearby.
